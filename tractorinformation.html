Top 5 Indian Tractor Companies Information

Mahindra & Mahindra (Mahindra)

Mahindra is the undisputed leader in the Indian tractor market, accounting for over 41% of the market share. They are known for their durable, technologically-advanced tractors catering to a wide range of agricultural needs.

Tractors and Farm Equipment Limited (TAFE)

TAFE is a large conglomerate comprising several tractor brands under its umbrella, including Massey Ferguson, Eicher, and TAFE. They offer a wide variety of tractors suitable for small, medium, and large farms.

Escorts Limited

Escorts is another major player in the Indian tractor market, renowned for its Powertrac, Farmtrac, and Digitrac brands. They focus on providing technologically-sound and fuel-efficient tractors for various agricultural applications.

Sonalika International Tractors Ltd. (Sonalika)

Sonalika is a leading tractor manufacturer known for its powerful and feature-rich tractors designed for diverse terrains and applications.

John Deere India Pvt. Ltd. (John Deere)

John Deere is a globally recognized agricultural machinery giant. Their Indian division offers a wide range of high-horsepower tractors equipped with advanced features catering to large-scale mechanized farming.
