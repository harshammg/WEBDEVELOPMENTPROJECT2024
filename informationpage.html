Top 5 Indian Car Companies Information

Maruti Suzuki

Maruti Suzuki is the undisputed leader of the Indian car market, accounting for over 42% of the market share. They are known for their fuel-efficient and affordable cars.

Hyundai Motors

Hyundai is a South Korean automaker that has become a major player in the Indian car market. They are known for their stylish and feature-packed vehicles.

Tata Motors

Tata Motors is a leading Indian automaker that offers a wide range of vehicles, from budget-friendly hatchbacks to luxurious SUVs.

Mahindra & Mahindra

Mahindra is a renowned Indian car company known for its rugged and capable SUVs. They also offer a variety of other vehicles, including hatchbacks, sedans, and pick-up trucks.

KIA

Kia is a South Korean automaker that is a relatively new entrant to the Indian car market. However, they have quickly gained popularity for their stylish and feature-rich vehicles.
